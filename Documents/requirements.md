# The Tactical Generalist — Requirements

## Project Overview
The Tactical Generalist is a personal project by Jeff Clawson — a tech executive, father of four, and self-described "jack of all trades" — designed to pass down practical life wisdom to his Gen Z children and others in their generation.

## Purpose
This is a legacy project, not a business. Wisdom preservation over monetization. Capturing 30+ years of lessons learned so they aren't lost when memory fades.

## Hub Website Role
The site serves as a central hub connecting all content across platforms, primarily YouTube (video guides) and Substack (written pieces and newsletters).

## Content Pillars
1. **Personal Finance** — Budgeting, investing, debt strategy, retirement planning
2. **Business & Career** — Networking, leadership, navigating corporate life
3. **Practical Life Skills** — Cooking techniques, home maintenance, travel tips, family recipes
4. **Philosophy & Mindset** — Gratitude, resilience, decision-making, maintaining perspective

## Target Audience
Young adults in their late teens through late twenties navigating the transition to full independence — though the advice is designed to be timeless and applicable to anyone building a life.

## Tone & Voice
- Direct, authentic, and grounded in real experience rather than theory
- First person ("I" not "we")
- Conversational — write like you're talking to your kids
- Practical over theoretical
- Humble — acknowledge uncertainty
- Encouraging without being patronizing
