Typography

A pairing of classic serif (Libre Baskerville) for headlines and a clean sans-serif (DM Sans) for body text. This combination balances timelessness with readability.

Display / Headlines — Libre Baskerville
Master Debt or You Will Become Its Slave
Weights: Regular (400), Bold (700), Italic (400)
Body / UI — DM Sans
Throughout my life I've seen people who I would consider affluent who were one paycheck away from the poorhouse and on the flip side, I know people so frugal you would never guess they have a significant net worth and could retire any time they wanted.
Weights: Regular (400), Medium (500), Semibold (600), Bold (700)

Voice & Tone

How The Tactical Generalist sounds—direct, warm, and grounded in real experience.

Attribute Description
Direct Get to the point. No fluff, no padding. Respect the reader's time.
Personal First person. Share real experiences and mistakes. "I" not "one should."
Practical Actionable over theoretical. "Here's what to do" not "here's what to consider."
Humble Acknowledge uncertainty. "This worked for me" not "this is the only way."
Encouraging Supportive without being patronizing. Assume intelligence, offer guidance.

✓ Do

  • Use conversational language
  • Share specific examples from your life
  • Acknowledge when you got it wrong
  • Give concrete, actionable steps
  • Write like you're talking to your kids

✕ Don't

  • Use jargon without explaining it
  • Preach or lecture
  • Promise guaranteed results
  • Be cynical or dismissive
  • Over-qualify every statement

Logo Usage

The TG compass logo represents direction and wisdom. The banner expands this with icons representing each pillar.

✓ Do

  • Use on dark backgrounds (charcoal, gunmetal)
  • Maintain clear space around the logo
  • Use the full-color version when possible
  • Scale proportionally

✕ Don't

  • Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Stretch or distort
  • Add effects (drop shadows, glows)
  • Recreate or modify the logo elements

File Formats Needed

  • TG_Logo.png — Square, transparent background (for profile pictures)
  • TG_Logo_Dark.png — On charcoal background (for light interfaces)
  • TG_Banner.png — Full width banner (2560 x 1440 for YouTube)
  • TG_Favicon.ico — 32x32 and 16x16 for browser tabs
